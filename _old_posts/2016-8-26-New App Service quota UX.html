Apps hosted in Free and Shared App Service plans get access to certain amounts of resources based on their quotas. You can learn more about app service quotas and how the apply to your apps here: How to: Monitor Apps in Azure App Service The new quota UX let’s get an at a glance view of the quotas applicable to your app. Each quota contains basic information about (1) the current utilization, (2) the limit for this specific quota, (3) when will the quota re-set and (4) the quota name. You can manually refresh the view by clicking on the refresh button in the action bar or provide us feedback directly about this new UX. This new UX makes it easier to understand the resource usage for your apps and what resources are being exhausted. If you have any questions about this UX or App Service in general be sure to check our forums in MSDN and Stack Overflow and for any feature requests or ideas check out our User Voice
