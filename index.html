Welcome to the Azure App Service team blog. Here you can find content from the Product and Engineering teams.

App Service is a managed hosting service for web apps and mobile back-ends. Quickly build, deploy, and scale web apps created with popular frameworks .NET, .NET Core, Node.js, Java, PHP, Ruby, or Python, in containers or running on any operating system. Meet rigorous, enterprise-grade performance, security, and compliance requirements by using the fully managed platform for your operational and monitoring tasks.
